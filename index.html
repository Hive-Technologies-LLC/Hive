About Hive

Hive was created to solve a fundamental gap in energy infrastructure: battery systems generate massive amounts of data, yet very little of it becomes usable intelligence. Inconsistent formats, vendor silos, and fragmented telemetry prevent operators from seeing the full picture.

HiveOS was built to unify and standardize battery analytics across hardware platforms — transforming raw telemetry into clear, structured health signals that enable smarter monitoring, predictive diagnostics, and scalable energy management.
